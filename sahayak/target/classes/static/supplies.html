Supplies for Different Calamities

Natural Calamities

  • Earthquake: First aid kits, emergency blankets, bottled water, non-perishable food.
  • Flood: Life jackets, sandbags, waterproof bags, emergency food supplies.
  • Wildfire: N95 masks, fire-resistant clothing, portable water containers, emergency food.

Man-Made Calamities

  • Chemical Spill: Chemical-resistant gloves, masks, emergency eye wash stations, neutralizing agents.
  • Power Outage: Flashlights, batteries, portable chargers, non-perishable food.
  • Terrorist Attack: First aid kits, emergency contact numbers, food and water supplies, protective gear.
