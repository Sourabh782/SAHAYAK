Welcome to Sahayak, a cutting-edge application designed to transform emergency response and rescue operations. In a world where timely assistance can make all the difference, Sahayak stands at the forefront of innovation, providing a comprehensive solution to real-time crisis management.

  • Real-Time Coordination: Integrates with emergency services for immediate updates and efficient responses.
  • Live Tracking: Uses GPS for precise location tracking and fast response.
  • Resource Management: Monitors supplies and personnel for optimal resource allocation.
  • Multi-Channel Communication: Enables instant communication among rescue teams, volunteers, and individuals.
